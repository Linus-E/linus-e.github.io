Home School Challenges

Here's some challenges if you're getting bored!

Prizes (total zeroed when prize chosen!)!

  • 100pts: a takeaway!
  • 300pts: day trip when all this is over + takeaway!
  • 750pts: nearish weekend trip when all this is over + two takeaways!
  • 1000pts: trip to theme park when all this is over + three takeaways!

Pairs (or more):

  1. Lock picking: at least two people learn how to pick a 3 chamber lock (10pts when both can do it)
